ABOUT

Sonu Nigam (born: 30 July 1973, Faridabad , Haryana , India ) is a famous singer of Hindi films. Apart from Hindi, he has also sung in Kannada , Oriya , Tamil , Assamese , Punjabi , Bengali , Marathi and Chhattisgarhi . He has made many indi-pop albums and worked in some Hindi films.Sonu sang his first song for Doordarshan's serial Talaash (1992), after which he entered Bollywood with the song O Aasmaan Wale Jami Par from the film Aaja Meri Jaan (1993) , then he started making advertisements on radio. He got big success when his song "Accha Sila Diya Tune Mere Pyar Ka" was released on 1 January 1993.
